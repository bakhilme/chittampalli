Who We Are

A Telangana Company
Trading with the World

Founded in Karimnagar, Chittampalli Exports Private Limited bridges India's abundant farmlands with global markets. We source directly from verified farms across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, applying rigorous grading and quality controls before every shipment.

Our regulatory registrations across FIEO, APEDA, FSSAI, IEC and the Spices Board ensure every consignment meets destination-country import standards — without surprises.

Direct Farm Sourcing

Traceable supply chains straight from farmer to freight — no middlemen.

Certified & Compliant

All five major agri-export certifications in place for smooth customs clearance.

End-to-End Logistics

Cold chain, freight, fumigation and documentation — fully managed.

Global Market Access

Established export lanes to Middle East, Europe, Southeast Asia and beyond.
