Company Overview

Chittampalli Exports Private Limited is a premier Indian agri-export company dedicated to delivering high-quality agricultural products to international markets. Based in Karimnagar, Telangana, we specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting fresh produce, spices, and cereals that meet the highest global standards.

With a commitment to quality, sustainability, and timely delivery, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for buyers across the globe seeking premium Indian agricultural products.
